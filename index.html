FAQ

Common Questions

Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely. Your resume, job data, and applications are stored locally on your machine. We never share your data with third parties. Your AI interactions use encrypted connections.

Does it actually submit applications automatically?

Job007 fills all application fields and uploads your tailored documents. Each application opens in a separate browser tab for your review. You control the final "Submit" click — or set it to fully autonomous mode.

What job platforms does it support?

LinkedIn, Indeed, Gmail job alerts, Glassdoor, and any company's career page you add to your "dream companies" list. We're constantly adding more sources.

How does the AI scoring work?

Our AI reads the full job description and compares it against your actual resume using semantic analysis — not simple keyword matching. It considers your skills, experience level, industry background, and career trajectory to produce a 0-100 match score.

Can I use it if I'm currently employed?

That's exactly who we built this for. Job007 runs on your schedule, quietly scanning and preparing applications. You spend zero hours on the search itself — just review what the agent found during your morning coffee.

What if I don't like a generated resume?

Every document is fully editable. You can also chat with the AI assistant and ask it to regenerate with specific instructions: "emphasize my leadership experience more" or "make the tone more confident." It learns your preferences over time.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — the Free plan is yours forever with 5 searches per day. No credit card required. When you're ready for unlimited searches and auto-apply, upgrade to Pro.
